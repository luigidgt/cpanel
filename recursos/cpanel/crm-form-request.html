    Integration

    You are requesting an MLS/IDX Integration with your board of realtors

    In order to integrate your real estate website with your MLS Board you must apply and be approved by the board. Our team will manage this process for you all the way until the deployment of your IDX Integration.

    Here is what to expect:
    • Submit your request for IDX Integration. Our team will validate your request with your MLS Boards.
    • Once approved we will activate the IDX Integration in your website and you will receive a notification by email.
    • Your billing cycle will begin one you accept the terms & conditions.
    • If there are any issues with your board of agent ID, you will not be billed until the integration is fully resolved.
    • If your board is not available for IDX Boost Integration you’ll be notified and will not be billed for the IDX Integration.
