Email Sender Setup / Privacy Policy

Email providers for your CRM

Your c-panel has certain functionalities like : property alerts, email drips, etc that needs your email provider for sending us those items. It also has the ability to read your email to extract the email from your lead to you. For this to happen we need to consume and communicate with Google API, Microsoft etc accordingly to your provider.

After the user is authenticated we use a refreshed token that is talking with your Google API everytime that we are composing an email or reading the user inbox.

Our data policy for the Google App

App does not collect any data from the email’s client, and we will not share any of this data for advertising purposes. We will not disclose any client’s confidential information.
